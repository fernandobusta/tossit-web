Our Mission

Our mission is based solely on the awareness of waste mismanagement. We provide both an interactive and educational resource for all ages. The aim of Toss It is to encourage proper waste disposal and recycling. We help the individual and the community make better informed recycling choices which in turn help save the plant. Toss It provides a simple solution to a growing problem.





Why Seeds For the Future

In the time we have been here, providing a solution to improve the sustainability of the planet has been our focus. We have learnt so much from our mentors, competitors and of course, our fantastic team.
