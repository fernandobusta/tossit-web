The App

The Toss It App has a scanner. The user scans the item and is informed whether or not they can recycle it. The scanner also has a feature that works on receipts. Once your receipt is scanned, the app analyses it and tells you what small changes you could make to your shopping list. This advice involves suggesting items with less packaging or items sourced locally. Finally, a rewards system is in place. We are currently working on teaming up with small Irish businesses in the hopes that they will get on board in being a part of our rewards system.

What makes us better than the competition:


  • AI integration
  • Gamification
  • Local Information
  • Available in Ireland
